FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find what you’re looking for?

Contact Us
1. Can I request a different Buddy if I’m not comfortable with the assigned one? Can I have the same Buddy for every visit?

Your comfort matters to us. If you’re not satisfied with your assigned Buddy, simply let us know with a valid reason, and we’ll promptly arrange a replacement. We also understand the importance of familiarity and bonding. While we’ll do our best to assign the same Buddy for each visit, if they’re unavailable, we’ll ensure a reliable and caring alternative.

2. Is Buddy assistance only available for members?

Once you sign up for any of our elder care plans, you gain access to multiple facilities. However, for services like property management, financial management, remote logistical support, educational transcript assistance, and legal advisory, membership is not required—you can book them anytime via call or WhatsApp.

3. How do I choose the right plan?

Our membership plans are designed based on extensive research into different stages of aging, mobility limitations, and emotional dependency. The level of care and support varies accordingly. Choosing the right plan depends on your loved one’s physical and emotional needs—what works for a 65-year-old may not be suitable for an 85-year-old. Our plans are structured to address these specific requirements.

4. Are you available 24/7? Can I call Buddy Back Home in case of a midnight emergency?

Yes, we are available 24/7, rain or storm. Your membership includes a dedicated emergency contact number—anyone can call us in case of a medical emergency, and we’ll be there to assist your loved one.

5. Can you help if my parents face issues with their mobile phone, technology, or potential fraud?

Absolutely. Every membership plan includes assistance with technology troubleshooting. If they encounter cybercrime or fraud, our team of 40+ legal experts is available to handle the situation, while we provide emotional support throughout.

6. Do you offer assistance during dialysis or chemotherapy visits?

Yes, we provide support during doctor visits, ensuring your loved ones feel safe and stable until they return home. For post-operative care, our Buddies offer consistent assistance through daily visits. They are also trained to monitor vital parameters when needed—just like you would if you were there. We have a 24*7 operating clinic called Care Now for regular and emergency medical support.

7. Can you assist if my parents want to relocate?

Yes, absolutely. We’ll handle everything from preparing their new home to packing, moving, and logistical support. We’ll also assist in setting up essentials like electricity bills, hiring household help, and arranging gas connections to ensure a smooth transition.

8. My father has dementia and is unable to withdraw his pension or do grocery shopping. Can you help?

Of course! We’ll take care of his bill payments, premiums, and accompany him for bank or post office visits. Since shopping might be overwhelming for him, we’ll handle grocery and fresh produce shopping once or twice a week during our visits. We’ll also assist in organizing everything at home to make daily life easier for him.
